MSTAR/IU (Public) Mixed Targets 15° & 45° + SLICY

      This CD contains synthetic aperture radar (SAR) image chips and the associated JPEG files of the 2S1, BRDM-2, BTR-60, D7, T62, ZIL-131, ZSU-23/4, and SLICY. The imagery on this disc was collected as part of the MSTAR Data Collection #1, Scene 1 and as part of the MSTAR Data Collection #2, Scenes 1, 2, and 3. The data was collected by Sandia National Laboratory (SNL) using the STARLOS sensor. The general collection parameters for the imagery on this disc are:
  • X-band
  • 15 and 45 degree depression angles
  • Spotlight mode
  • 1-foot resolution
  • SLICY data has these additional depression angles available:
    16, 29, 31, 43, 44.
